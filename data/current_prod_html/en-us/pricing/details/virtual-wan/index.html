Virtual WAN Virtual WAN

Simple, unified, global connectivity and security

Azure Virtual WAN is a networking service providing optimized and automated branch to branch connectivity through Azure. Virtual WAN allows customers to connect branches to each other and Azure, centralizing their network and security needs with virtual appliances such as firewalls and Azure network and security services.

FAQ

Expand All
  • Is a partial hour billed as a full hour?

    Yes. A partial hour is billed as a full hour.

  • Are data transfers over the VPN connection charged separately?

    Data transfers over the VPN connections to your on-premises sites or the internet in general are charged separately at the regular data transfer rate.
