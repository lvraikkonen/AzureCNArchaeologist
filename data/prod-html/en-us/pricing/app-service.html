SSL Connections

App Service supports two types of SSL connections: Server Name Indication (SNI) SSL connections and IP address SSL connections. SNI-based SSL is suitable for modern browsers while IP-based SSL is suitable for all browsers.

There is no charge for using SNI-based SSL. One IP SSL can be used with the Standard and Premium service plans at no additional charge. The Free and Shared service plans do not support SSL. You can purchase the right to use more SSL connections at the prices below. In all cases, the SSL certificate itself must be purchased separately.

FAQ

Expand all
  • Do I need to pay for apps in stopped state?

    Yes. The rates listed apply to apps in stopped state.

    For Shared instances, please delete apps that are not in use, or update their tier to Free to avoid charges.

    For Standard/Premium Service Plan instances, please delete corresponding app service plan and the instances to avoid charges.

  • Do you offer SSL connections for the default “chinacloudsites.cn” domain?

    Yes, App Service provides SSL connections for all URLs under the “chinacloudsites.cn” domain at no additional charge. You can use https://chinacloudsites.cn to access your apps securely.

  • Do you provide SSL certificates for custom domains?

    No. You can add your own SSL certificates for use with apps/websites when using SSL connections for custom domains. Self-signed and third party certificate issuing authority certificates are both supported.

  • Are multiple SSL certificates supported?

    Yes. However, please note that SSL connections for each certificate are billed separately. We also support SAN/UC certificates which enable you to secure multiple domains using a single SSL certificate.

  • Does App Service include SQL?

    App Service offerings include access to a free 20mb SQL database for 12 months. After 12 months, the free SQL Database converts to a paid basic SQL subscription. If you choose not to use SQL with App Service then the paid basic subscription is not required after 12 months.
